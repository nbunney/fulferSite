Protecting Your Interests

Generations of Central Valley residents have relied on the Fulfer name when needing a probate attorney or legal counsel. E. Paul Fulfer opened the practice in the 1950s, and was later joined by his son, Richard, who has continued to serve clients since 1980. The practice is built on honesty, ethics, and integrity.

At the Law Office of Richard L. Fulfer, clients come first. Every client whether their needs are for probate, family law, personal injury, criminal, or DUI is treated with respect and guaranteed effective representation. It is Richard's goal that every client will receive the best outcome for his/her case. With over 35 years of extensive experience, Richard is well prepared to provide his clients with aggressive, effective, and compassionate counsel.
